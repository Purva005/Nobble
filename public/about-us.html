Our achievement

We’ve grown with purpose. From a humble beginning to becoming a trusted name in hyperlocal services — here’s our journey so far:

Jan 2024

Launched the Idea

Started with a vision to simplify access to home services across India. Built a small but passionate team to validate the problem.

Mar 2024

MVP & Beta Testing

Released a working prototype (web + app) with essential services like electrician, cleaning, and beauty at home. Beta-tested in 2 cities with 500+ bookings.

May 2024

Partner Onboarding

Onboarded 100+ verified service professionals and launched training + background verification processes to ensure quality and safety.

Aug 2024

App Launched

Released stable version of Android & iOS apps. Added advanced features like real-time tracking, in-app payments, reviews, and support chat.

Jan 2025

50K+ Users

Expanded to 10 cities, crossed 50,000 registered users and 10,000+ successful bookings. Started partnerships with local vendors for growth.

June 2025

Brand Recognition & Funding

Featured in startup media. Secured seed funding. Recognized as one of the most promising hyperlocal platforms solving real-world problems at scale.
